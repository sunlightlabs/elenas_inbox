     promote the value of respecting diversity in schools and communities. The forum

     including U.S. Gold Medalist Carl Lewis, former Georgetown basketball star Dikembe

Environmental Protection Agency

Civil Rights Act Implementation Advisory Committee. On May 18-19, EPA's
advisory committee on implementation of Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 met
for the first time. Administrator Browner established this new advisory committee in
March to provide recommendations on how federal government funding recipients can
effectively comply with Title VI ofthe Civil Rights Act. The new advisory committee
will evaluate and recommend techniques to assess Title VI environmental justice
concerns and, where they exist, identify avenues to address them before they become the
subject of complaints.
