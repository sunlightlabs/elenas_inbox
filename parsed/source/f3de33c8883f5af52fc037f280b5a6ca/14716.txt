Contribution Limits
45 states limit contributions to campaigns by individuals, PACs, corporations or unions. A 1994 survey
conducted for the Conference on Campaign Reform showed that of the 1,808 people surveyed, 54
supported in-district contribution limits, which would require candidates to receive most of their
donations from sources within their district.

Income Tax Check-o(fs and Add-ons

because it uses the tax system to solicit small, voluntary contributions that are distributed according to a

Participation rates increase when a tax check-off system is used.
