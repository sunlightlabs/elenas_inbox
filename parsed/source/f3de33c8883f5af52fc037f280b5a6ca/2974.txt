  SUBJECT:           LEGISLATIVE REPORT

  John A. Koskinen, Deputy Director for Management, will testify Wednesday,
  June 18, before the Senate Subcommittee on Oversight of Government
  Management, Restructuring, and the District of Columbia of the
  Governmental Affairs Committee regarding S. 314, "The Freedom from
  Government Competition Act" and competitive contracting within the Federal
  government.

  Jacob J. Lew, Deputy Director, will testify Thursday, June 19, before the
  House Subcommittee on Public Buildings and Economic Development of the
  Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure regarding budget scoring
  rules as they apply to real estate transactions.

  RECONCILIATION:
  The Senate Finance Committee meets on Tuesday to markup the spending
  provisions in the reconciliation bill. We expect Senator Roth to lay down
  a spending mark that covers all of the spending provisions -- Medicare,


  Medicaid, Kids' Health, Unemployment Insurance, Immigration, and
  Welfare.   After opening remarks in the morning, the entire mark will be
  open to amendments beginning at 2 in the afternoon.

  Also on Tuesday, the Senate Government Affairs Committee will markup
  provisions regarding Civil Service, Postal Service, Governor's Island,
  and Air Rights.  In addition, the Commerce Committee will meet to markup
  the Spectrum and Vessel Tonnage provisions.
