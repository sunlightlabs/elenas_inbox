    bipartisan cooperation:

   The Chemical Weapons Convention has been a model of bipartisan cooperation. First
    negotiated under President Reagan, completed under President Bush and submitted to the

    Democrats -- including Senator Dole, General Powell, Brent Scowcroft and military
