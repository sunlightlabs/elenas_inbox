                                   Fact Sheet on the Uninsured


General Statistics

                                                   l
      40.3 million Americans are uninsured.
                                                                                                         2
      Fully 85% (34 million) of the uninsured are workers or are in families of workers.

Uninsured Children Statistics

      Of the 40 million uninsured, 10 million are children. 3

      Of that, 80% (8 million) of the uninsured children have a parent who is a worker. 4

      Of the 10 million uninsured children, about 3 million are eligible for Medicaid but are not
       currentlyenrolled. 5

Statistical Back-Up for Workers In-Between Jobs

      Almost 3 out of 5 people who lose their health insurance do so because of a change in
       their employment. 6

      45% of the children who lose their health insurance do so due to a change of employment
       of their parents. 7
